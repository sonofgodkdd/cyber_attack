Date: October 08, 2010
8th Annual Bi-National Red Ribbon Rally
OCT 08  -- 
Joseph M. Arabit, Special Agent in Charge (SAC) of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA), El Paso Field Division, and Mary Ellen Hernandez, Executive Director of the Rio Grande Safe Communities Coalition, would like to invite you to the 8 th Annual Bi-National Red Ribbon Rally on Saturday, October 9, 2010. In a brief ceremony at approximately 2:00 p.m., SAC Arabit will share the history of the Red Ribbon Campaign, and it's inspiration through the life and sacrifice of Special Agent (SA) Enrique "Kiki" Camarena. During this same ceremony, "Drug Prevention Officers of the Year" from local law enforcement agencies will be recognized.
